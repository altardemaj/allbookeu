Privacy Policy

How AllBookEU handles your information

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

AllBookEU helps guests discover and book restaurants in Kosovo and Albania. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to operate the booking platform.

Information We Collect

When you use AllBookEU, we may collect account details such as your name, email address, phone number, password hash, booking history, restaurant preferences, reviews, and messages or notes you choose to add to a reservation.

For restaurant owners, we may collect business profile information including restaurant name, address, phone number, email address, opening hours, table setup, reservation settings, and owner account details.

How We Use Information

We use information to create and manage accounts, process reservations, notify guests and restaurants, support restaurant operations, improve the service, prevent abuse, and communicate important account or booking updates.

Sharing Information

Reservation details are shared with the restaurant you book so they can prepare for your visit. We may also use service providers for hosting, database storage, email delivery, analytics, and security. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage and Security

AllBookEU uses hosted infrastructure providers to store and process platform data. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no online service can guarantee perfect security.

Your Choices

You can update your account information from your profile page. You may request account deletion or data questions by contacting us at support@allbookeu.com.

Children

AllBookEU is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Changes

We may update this policy as AllBookEU grows. If changes are material, we will update the date above and may provide additional notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@allbookeu.com.
